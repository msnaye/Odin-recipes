Alfredo

This is the greatest shrimp alfredo recipe you will ever taste. It is fingerlicking good and easy to make.

Go Back Home

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo-style pasta sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta
  • 2 pounds fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup of melted butter
  • 1/2 small onions (finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions

Step 1

  1. In a saucepan over low-temperature, heat the Alfredo sauce. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Step 2

  1. Boil shrimp in a large pot of water until they turn orange. Then place in bowl with melted butter. Let shrimp marinate for 15 to 30 minutes; remove. In a large skillet over medium heat, saute the green pepper and onion in a small amount of oil.

Step 3

  1. Mix together the cooked pasta, shrimp, pepper-onion mixture and Alfredo sauce. Season with garlic powder and cumin.
